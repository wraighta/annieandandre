Our Summer Adventures

How it all started...

Annie and Andre met in 2016, and quickly found they had a lot in common. They love video games, board games, card games... just games in general. But sometimes, they take a break from gaming and go explore the world...

First trip was a getaway to the exotic Sandusky, Ohio to spend 2 full days at Cedar Point Amusment Park. Annie decided to ride the fastest, biggest, newest ride in the park, and regreted it almost instantly.

Being a short drive away from Toronto, it seemed only natural that they would end up there. They did what the tourists do - A walk around the CN Tower, wandering the streets near Eatons Centre, and taking in a Blue Jays Game.

In 2019, Annie and Andre toured the scenic Niagara falls. They spend most of their time on Clifton Hill, and got a chance to see Marineland as well.
